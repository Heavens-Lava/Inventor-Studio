# Goals App Documentation

## Purpose

A comprehensive app for setting, planning, and tracking major and minor goals. The app helps users break down ambitions into actionable steps, monitor progress, and stay motivated.

## Core Features

- **Major & Minor Goals:** Create, organize, and prioritize both large and small goals.
- **Planning Area:** Outline steps, milestones, and strategies for each goal.
- **Costs Area:** Estimate and track financial, time, or resource costs for goals.
- **Time It Takes Area:** Set and visualize estimated timeframes for completion.
- **Feedback Area:** Add notes, reflections, and receive feedback from self or others.
- **Progress Tracker:** Visualize progress with charts, checklists, or timelines.
- **Tools Needed Area:** List and link resources, apps, or materials required.
- **Scope Area:** Define the scope, boundaries, and impact of each goal.

## Advanced Feature Recommendations

- **Subgoals & Tasks:** Break goals into actionable subgoals and tasks.
- **Reminders & Notifications:** Alert users about deadlines, milestones, or progress updates.
- **Collaboration:** Share goals, plans, or progress with others for accountability.
- **Templates:** Pre-made goal templates for common ambitions (fitness, learning, finance, etc.).
- **Integration:** Link goals to tasks, calendar events, habits, or journal entries in the suite.
- **Progress Analytics:** Insights into goal achievement rates, time spent, and bottlenecks.
- **Motivational Widgets:** Display quotes, tips, or encouragement.
- **Customizable Layouts:** Choose from different planning and tracking views.
- **Export/Import:** Save goals as PDF, text, or images; import from other tools.
- **Multi-device Sync:** Access goals across mobile and desktop.
- **Offline Support:** Use the app without internet, sync when online.
- **Accessibility:** High-contrast mode, screen reader support, keyboard navigation.
- **Dark Mode:** Switch between light and dark themes.
- **AI Assistance:** Suggest goal breakdowns, track progress, or recommend strategies.
- **Undo/Redo Actions:** Easily revert or repeat changes in plans or progress.
- **Privacy Controls:** Set goals as private, shared, or public.

## Design Notes

- Prioritize clarity, motivation, and ease of use
- Use smooth animations and transitions
- Support drag-and-drop for planning and organizing goals
- Allow users to personalize their experience
