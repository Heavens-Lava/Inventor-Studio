# Habits App Documentation

## Purpose

A powerful app for building, tracking, and improving habits and routines. The app helps users develop effective behaviors, monitor progress, and achieve personal growth in areas like productivity, wellness, and finance.

## Core Features

- **Routine Builder:** Create and organize daily, weekly, or custom routines.
- **Progress Tracking:** Visualize habit streaks, completions, and trends over time.
- **Financial Habits:** Track and encourage good financial behaviors (e.g., saving, budgeting).
- **Time Spent Tracking:** Monitor and analyze time spent on each habit.
- **Reminders & Notifications:** Set custom alerts to encourage consistency.
- **Habit Notes:** Add reflections, tips, or notes to each habit.
- **Customizable Layouts:** Choose from different tracking and visualization views.
- **Tags & Categories:** Organize habits by type (health, productivity, finance, etc.).

## Advanced Feature Recommendations

- **AI Habit Suggestions:** Recommend new habits based on user goals or activity.
- **Flexible Scheduling:** Support for non-daily habits and custom intervals.
- **Calendar Integration:** Sync habit completions with a calendar view.
- **Motivational Widgets:** Display quotes, tips, or encouragement.
- **Gamification:** Earn badges, points, or rewards for maintaining streaks.
- **Collaboration:** Share habits or progress with friends for accountability.
- **Templates:** Pre-made habit templates for quick setup.
- **Search & Filter:** Find habits by keyword, tag, or category.
- **Multi-device Sync:** Access habits across mobile and desktop.
- **Offline Support:** Use the app without internet, sync when online.
- **Accessibility:** High-contrast mode, screen reader support, keyboard navigation.
- **Dark Mode:** Switch between light and dark themes.
- **Data Export/Import:** Backup or transfer habit data.
- **Undo/Redo Actions:** Easily revert or repeat changes in habit tracking.
- **Privacy Controls:** Set habits as private, shared, or public.

## Design Notes

- Prioritize motivation, clarity, and ease of use
- Use smooth animations and transitions
- Support drag-and-drop for organizing routines
- Allow users to personalize their experience
