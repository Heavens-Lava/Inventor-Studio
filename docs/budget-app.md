# Budget App Documentation

## Purpose

A comprehensive app for managing personal finances, creating budgets, and tracking income and expenses. The app helps users plan, monitor, and optimize their spending to achieve financial goals.

## Core Features

- **Income Tracking:** Enter and manage biweekly, weekly, monthly, and yearly income.
- **Budget Creation:** Automatically generate budgets for weekly, biweekly, monthly, and yearly periods by subtracting expenses from income.
- **Expense Integration:** Import or sync expenses from the Daily Expense Tracker and other sources.
- **Budget Categories:** Allocate funds to categories (food, bills, savings, entertainment, etc.).
- **Budget Comparison:** Compare planned vs. actual spending for each period.
- **Budget Adjustments:** Edit and update budgets as income or expenses change.

## Advanced Feature Recommendations

- **Savings Goals:** Set and track savings targets within budgets.
- **Forecasting:** Predict future budgets and cash flow based on historical data.
- **Alerts & Notifications:** Receive alerts for overspending, upcoming bills, or budget milestones.
- **Visualization:** Charts and graphs for budget breakdowns, trends, and progress.
- **Recurring Income & Expenses:** Automatically handle regular income and bills.
- **Multi-device Sync:** Access budgets across mobile and desktop.
- **Offline Support:** Use the app without internet, sync when online.
- **Data Export/Import:** Export budgets as CSV, PDF, or images; import from other tools.
- **Integration:** Link budgets to finance app, expense tracker, calendar, or goals in the suite.
- **Accessibility:** High-contrast mode, screen reader support, keyboard navigation.
- **Dark Mode:** Switch between light and dark themes.
- **AI Assistance:** Suggest budget optimizations, analyze spending, and forecast savings.
- **Motivational Widgets:** Display financial tips, encouragement, or savings quotes.
- **Undo/Redo Actions:** Easily revert or repeat changes in budget planning.
- **Privacy Controls:** Set budgets as private, shared, or public.

## Design Notes

- Prioritize clarity, ease of use, and actionable insights
- Use smooth animations and transitions
- Support drag-and-drop for organizing budget categories
- Allow users to personalize their experience
