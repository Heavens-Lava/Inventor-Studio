# Ideas App Documentation

## Purpose

A creative board for users to capture, organize, and develop ideas visually and interactively. The app empowers brainstorming, planning, and innovation with rich features and seamless collaboration.

## Core Features

- **Idea Board:** Add, edit, and organize ideas as cards or sticky notes.
- **Drawing Tools:** Freehand drawing, shapes, arrows, and connectors for visual brainstorming.
- **Image Support:** Upload and embed images, screenshots, or sketches.
- **Text & Thoughts:** Add detailed notes, comments, and thoughts to each idea.
- **Arrows & Connectors:** Link ideas visually to show relationships and flow.
- **Brainstorming Mode:** Timed or free brainstorming sessions with idea grouping.
- **Customizable Layouts:** Grid, freeform, or mind map layouts.
- **Color & Tagging:** Assign colors, tags, or categories to ideas for organization.

## Advanced Feature Recommendations

- **Collaboration:** Real-time multi-user editing and commenting.
- **Templates:** Pre-made boards for common brainstorming scenarios (e.g., SWOT, mind map, Kanban).
- **Voting & Feedback:** Allow users to vote or comment on ideas.
- **Version History:** Track changes and revert to previous board states.
- **Export/Import:** Save boards as images, PDFs, or shareable links; import from other tools.
- **Integration:** Link ideas to tasks, calendar events, or journal entries in the suite.
- **Search & Filter:** Find ideas by keyword, tag, or category.
- **Mobile & Desktop Friendly:** Responsive design for all devices.
- **Offline Support:** Use the app without internet, sync when online.
- **Accessibility:** High-contrast mode, screen reader support, keyboard navigation.
- **Dark Mode:** Switch between light and dark themes.
- **AI Assistance:** Suggest idea improvements, related concepts, or help organize boards.
- **Audio Notes:** Record and attach voice memos to ideas.
- **Undo/Redo Actions:** Easily revert or repeat changes on the board.
- **Privacy Controls:** Set boards as private, shared, or public.

## Design Notes

- Prioritize creativity, flexibility, and ease of use
- Use smooth animations and transitions
- Support drag-and-drop for arranging ideas
- Allow users to personalize their experience
