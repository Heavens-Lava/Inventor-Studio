# Daily Expense Tracker App Documentation

## Purpose

A user-friendly app for tracking daily expenses, analyzing spending habits, and supporting smarter financial decisions. The app helps users monitor costs, visualize trends, and discover opportunities to save money.

## Core Features

- **Daily Expense Tracking:** Log daily spending by category (food, gas, hobbies, bills, etc.).
- **Monthly & Yearly Calculations:** Automatically calculate monthly and estimated yearly expenses based on daily data.
- **Category Breakdown:** View spending by category with charts and summaries.
- **Food Consumption Tracking:** Record daily food expenses and analyze trends.
- **Gas & Transportation Tracking:** Track daily spending on gas and transportation.
- **Hobbies & Entertainment Tracking:** Monitor spending on hobbies, entertainment, and leisure.
- **Bills & Recurring Expenses:** Track regular bills and subscriptions.
- **Custom Categories:** Add and manage custom expense categories.

## Advanced Feature Recommendations

- **Savings Assistant:** Suggest ways to save money and calculate long-term savings from reducing spending in specific categories.
- **Budget Planning:** Set daily, weekly, or monthly budgets and receive alerts when approaching limits.
- **Spending Insights:** Visualize trends, averages, and anomalies in spending habits.
- **Goal Setting:** Set savings goals and track progress toward them.
- **Expense Forecasting:** Predict future expenses based on historical data.
- **Receipt & Image Upload:** Attach receipts or images to expense entries.
- **Recurring Expense Detection:** Automatically identify and track recurring expenses.
- **Multi-device Sync:** Access expense data across mobile and desktop.
- **Offline Support:** Log and view expenses without internet, sync when online.
- **Data Export/Import:** Export expense data as CSV, PDF, or images; import from other tools.
- **Integration:** Link expenses to finance app, calendar events, or goals in the suite.
- **Accessibility:** High-contrast mode, screen reader support, keyboard navigation.
- **Dark Mode:** Switch between light and dark themes.
- **AI Assistance:** Analyze spending, suggest optimizations, and forecast savings.
- **Motivational Widgets:** Display savings tips, encouragement, or financial quotes.
- **Undo/Redo Actions:** Easily revert or repeat changes in expense tracking.
- **Privacy Controls:** Set expense data as private, shared, or public.

## Design Notes

- Prioritize clarity, ease of use, and actionable insights
- Use smooth animations and transitions
- Support drag-and-drop for organizing expenses and categories
- Allow users to personalize their experience
